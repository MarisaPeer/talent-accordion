Virtual Classrooms

Attending the Virtual Classroom days provides you with the opportunity to practice building your skills, with support and feedback from our team of professional trainers. You will also develop significant relationships with your peers as you continue to learn and practice together. All 4 days of the Virtual Classrooms run independently. You can choose when you want to attend each Virtual Classroom (Day 1, Day 2, Day 3 and Day 4) based on how you progress through the program and the dates/times that work best for you.

Virtual Classroom Day 1 focuses on the communications skills and strategies you will need to deliver a great Intake and Consultation. This will set you and your clients up for a strong start.

Virtual Classroom Day 2 centres around setting the client up for success when delivering RTT® through Zoom/Skype, including practicing the Induction and Suggestibility.

Virtual Classroom Day 3 will focus on constructing and delivering a perfect Reframe and Transformation.

Virtual Classroom Day 4 focuses on RFPI and Command Cell Therapy. (Please note this can only be booked once it has been covered in Module 6).

You can find upcoming dates and book Virtual Classroom days by accessing the your My RTT® Portal. All virtual events are registration only.
